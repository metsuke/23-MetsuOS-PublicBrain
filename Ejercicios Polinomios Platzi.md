## (2x+y)(3x-8y)
 $$ (2x+y)(3x-8y) => ((2x*3x) + (16xy) + (3xy) + (-8y^2)) => 6x^2 - 16xy + 3xy -8y^2 => 6x^2 - 8y^2 + 13xy  $$
